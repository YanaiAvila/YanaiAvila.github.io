Plant Health Monitoring and Alert System

Course: Internet of Things
Boards Used: Arduino MKR1000 (board overview)
Language: C++ (Arduino)
Project Description: For my final project, I implemented an environmental monitoring and alert system designed to ensure optimal plant health in indoor setups. It monitors 2 environmental conditions that are important to the health of a plant: light levels and soil moisture.
The system provides real-time feedback though the Blynk interface and it also triggers physical indicators. LEDs and buzzers are used to signal critical conditions.
