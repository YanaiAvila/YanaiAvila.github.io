About Me

I recently graduated from the University of Nevada, Las Vegas with a B.S. in Computer Engineering.

I was born and raised in Las Vegas, NV. I played the guitar and vihuela for 7 years of my life in both my middle school and high school Mariachi groups. Towards the end of high school, I decided to join the robotics team and that is when I realized that I wanted to be an engineer. I quickly learned that I love the problem solving part of engineering. I like figuring things out and tinkering. I tried my best to take classes that were very hands on in college, and circuits, IOT, embedded systems, and robotics were among my favorites.

I hope to gain experience with robotics and entertainment engineering as these are the things that make me excited about engineering.
