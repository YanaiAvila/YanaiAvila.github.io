About Me

I recently graduated from the University of Nevada, Las Vegas with a B.S. in Computer Engineering . It took a bit of figuring things out to get to this point in my life, but here we are.

I was born and raised in Las Vegas, NV. I played the guitar and vihuela for 7 years of my life in both my middle school and high school Mariachi groups. Towards the end of high shool, I decided to join the robotics team and that is when I realized that I was meant to be an engineer instead of doing music for the rest of my life. And so I chose engineering. I quickly learned that I love the problem solving part of engineering. I like figuring things out and tinkering. I tried my best to take classes that were very hands on in college, and circuits, IOT, embedded systems, and robotics were among my favorites.
